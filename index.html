THE ENGAGEMENT PROBLEM

Treatment software often reacts after the patient has already left.

Digital therapeutics can deliver structured treatment at scale, but most follow a fixed path. A missed session may trigger a reminder, yet the system rarely understands the gradual pattern that led to disengagement.

Bazi analyzes changes across completion, timing, duration, fatigue, and treatment difficulty so therapeutic products can respond before a temporary decline becomes abandonment.

HOW BAZI WORKS

A closed-loop intelligence layer for software-based care.

01

Observe behavior

Bazi receives product signals such as completion, session length, response timing, fatigue, and difficulty feedback.

02

Estimate risk

The system identifies sustained behavioral change and estimates the probability of near-term disengagement.

03

Select an intervention

Bazi evaluates only the actions approved by the therapeutic provider, such as pacing, reminders, or escalation.

04

Adapt and learn

The product updates the treatment experience and measures whether engagement improves.
