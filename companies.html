FROM STATIC TO RESPONSIVE

The treatment path should not remain fixed when the patient experience changes.

WITHOUT BAZI Static treatment workflow
Treatment begins

The patient receives a fixed sequence of sessions, exercises and reminders.

Engagement declines

Sessions become shorter, later and less frequent.

Generic reminder sent

The treatment experience itself remains unchanged.

Patient leaves treatment

The product reacts only after meaningful engagement has already been lost.

WITH BAZI Adaptive treatment workflow
Treatment begins

The patient receives the configured therapeutic experience.

Engagement changes

Bazi observes changes across approved behavioral and treatment signals.

Risk identified

Bazi recognizes a sustained pattern before complete disengagement.

Approved adaptation delivered

The product adjusts pacing, timing, support or escalation within defined limits.
