HOW BAZI WORKS

A closed-loop intelligence layer for software-based care.

01

Observe behavior

Bazi receives signals such as completion, response timing, session duration, reported fatigue and treatment difficulty.

02

Estimate risk

The platform identifies sustained behavioral change and estimates the probability of near-term disengagement.

03

Select a bounded response

Bazi evaluates only actions approved by the therapeutic organization, provider or clinical team.

04

Adapt and learn

The therapeutic product delivers the approved adaptation and measures whether meaningful engagement improves.

THE RESEARCH INFORMING BAZI

Meaningful engagement must be understood, measured and improved responsibly.

Bazi is being developed around a straightforward premise: digital treatment cannot deliver its intended value when patients do not remain meaningfully engaged.
