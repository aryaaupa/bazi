THE PRODUCT PROBLEM

Most therapeutic software follows a fixed path. Patients do not.

Digital therapeutics can deliver structured interventions at scale, but many products still respond to disengagement only after participation has already declined.

A missed session may trigger another reminder. A second missed session may trigger the same reminder again. The product records the behavior, but does not necessarily interpret the pattern.

Bazi is designed to convert developing behavioral change into a constrained, reviewable recommendation.

01 / TREATMENT

Fixed pathway

The same pacing, timing and support are delivered to every patient state.

02 / CHANGE

Behavior shifts

Completion, timing, duration or reported difficulty begins to change.

03 / REACTION

Generic reminder

The system reacts to an isolated event rather than the emerging pattern.

04 / LOSS

Disengagement grows

Participation continues to decline without a more relevant response.

05 / OUTCOME

Patient leaves

Completion, outcome measurement and product value become harder to demonstrate.

PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE

Integrate adaptive decisioning without rebuilding the patient experience.

Bazi is designed to sit behind the existing therapeutic interface. Your product continues to own treatment content, clinical workflow, patient communication and the final delivery of approved interventions.

Bazi evaluates behavioral context and returns a constrained recommendation that can be reviewed, logged and delivered according to your organization’s rules.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What product and clinical teams should know.

Does Bazi diagnose patients?

No. Bazi is not designed to determine whether a patient has a medical condition.

Does Bazi prescribe treatment?

No. The system evaluates only the actions supplied and approved by the therapeutic organization or clinical team.

Do we need to rebuild our product?

Bazi is designed to operate behind the existing therapeutic experience through structured data exchange and a recommendation interface.

Who controls the available interventions?

The therapeutic company, provider or clinical team defines the approved action library and any required review rules.

Has Bazi established clinical efficacy?

Not yet. Bazi is in an early product-development stage and has not established clinical efficacy for its platform.

What type of partners is Bazi seeking?

Bazi is seeking conversations with digital therapeutic companies, virtual care platforms, researchers and clinical teams interested in adaptive engagement workflows.
